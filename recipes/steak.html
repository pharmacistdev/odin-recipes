Reverse Sear Steak

Description

Easy reverse seared steak recipe. Meat -> Oven -> Pan -> Stomach

Ingredients

  • Thick cut steak
  • Kosher salt / fresh ground pepper
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Generously season steaks with salt and pepper.
  2. Preheat oven between 200/275. Set the steak on a wire rack in a rimmed baking sheet.
  3. Place steaks in the oven and cook until the internal temperature reaching 125F.
  4. Add 1mL of oil to cast iron skillet and heat. Add the steaks to the skillet to brown.
