Cheeseburger Pizza

Description

Combine two favorite foods into one, peak efficiency.

Ingredients

  • Cooking Spray
  • Pre-made pizza dough
  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Cheddar Cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F. Grease baking sheet with cooking spray. Unroll the pizza dough over the baking sheet.
  2. Bake this into a preheated oven, for around 8 minutes.
  3. Cook/stir beef and onion into a skillet until the beef is browned. Approximately 5-7 minutes
  4. Remove the pizza from the oven. Place the pasta sauce, cheese and ground beef mixture on the pizza. Then return to the oven until the crust is golden (~6 - 10 minutes)
