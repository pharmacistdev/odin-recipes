Lasagna

Description

A lovely lasagna that even Garfield would love!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Dry Lasagna Pasta
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Cheddar Cheese

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add Dry Lasagna to the boiling water and cook for 8-10 minutes.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In a large skillet, brown beef and season with salt and pepper.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine Mozzarella, Cheddar and Ricotta. In a pan, alternate layers of noddles, meat mixture and cheese mixture.
  4. Bake in a preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted.
